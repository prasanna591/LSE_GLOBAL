WHO WE ARE

Luminen Service for Excellence

Founded by Venkatesh M., LSE has been the cornerstone of career transformation in South India

Our Story

Since 2007, LSE has pioneered innovative training methodologies that bridge the gap between academic learning and corporate excellence. We've established a strong presence across schools, colleges, and corporate sectors in Pondicherry and South Arcot.

Our CLAP program prepares students for today's competitive corporate landscape through practical, activity-based learning that goes beyond traditional education.

  • Activity-Based Learning Methodology (ABLM)
  • Practical Workshop-Driven Training
  • Industry-Expert Faculty
  • Proven Placement Track Record
  • Personalized Career Guidance

Our Mission

To empower the unemployed and underemployed by transforming every student into a highly employable professional who understands their unique potential and strengths.

Our Vision

Combining the restless energy of youth with the priceless wisdom of experience to create an atmosphere where every student thrives and excels.
